About the Artist

At my core, I am three things: an artist, a rebel, and an entrepreneur. Creativity and curiosity have always been my greatest loves, yet for much of my journey, I felt confined by structures that dictated how I should express them.

The roads laid out for me always seemed too narrow, leaving little room to be truly free. Music has always been my first love—the sound, the why, the how—it is a part of me. Yet, the world of music has been changing. How it is made, how it is shared, and how it is valued all look different now. My place in that world never felt quite right, as if something was missing. I realized I didn’t want to be just another musician; I wanted to question, to innovate, and to reimagine the role of music and art in a .shifting culture.

For me, this means breaking barriers, redefining what is possible, and creating spaces where freedom and creativity have no limits. With Web3 and NFTs, the ordinary person now has the power to shape their own destiny. I see that my identity as an artist, rebel, and entrepreneur is not separate from this shift—it is aligned with it. My work is about finding freedom within creativity, .challenging narrow roads, and embracing technologies that open wider horizons .
