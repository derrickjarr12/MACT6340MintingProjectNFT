Welcome to NFT Mint

Derrick Jarrell

At my core, I am three things: an artist, a rebel, and an entrepreneur. Creativity and curiosity have always been my greatest loves, yet for much of my journey, I felt confined by structures that dictated how I should express them.

Music has always been my first love—the sound, the why, the how—it is a part of me. Yet, the world of music has been changing. I realized I didn’t want to be just another musician; I wanted to question, to innovate, and to reimagine the role of music and art in a shifting culture.
