About the Artist

At my core, I am three things: an artist, a rebel, and an entrepreneur. Creativity and curiosity have always been my greatest loves, yet for much of my journey, I felt confined by structures that dictated how I should express them.

The roads laid out for me always seemed too narrow, leaving little room to truly be free. Music has always been my first love the sound, the why, the how it is a part of me. But the world of music has changed. How it is made, shared, and valued looks entirely different now.Now is the time.This is about breaking barriers, redefining what is possible, and creating space where freedom and creativity know no limits. In a world that has lost its sense of wonder, I believe that questioning and reimagining music and art is how you shift culture.
